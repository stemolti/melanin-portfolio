My name is Fernanda, I am 23 years old and I am from Colombia. I am studying Graphic design at LABA academy in Brescia, Italy. The reason why I have chosen this course of study is because I wanted to combine it with my passion for fashion photography and photography in general, which I have always had since I was a kid. All my childhood life has been documented by my father who always had a little camera in his pocket. Photography to me means freedom and imagination.
